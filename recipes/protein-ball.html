Protein Balls

Recipe

Protein balls are little bite of peanut buttery goodness that will keep you going until mealtime. ALso known as prtein bites or energy balls, the combination of nut butter, oats, and flax or protein powder makes for a high-energy, tasty snack. Their flavor and texture will remind you of a chewy peanut butter cookie, but without any refined sugar,flour, or butter.

You won't need any special equipment to make these energy bites. Just a bowl and a wooden spoon or spatual-no food processor required. If you'd like to include protein powder, swap it out for the gound flax and chia seeds. Plain, vanilla, or chocolate protein powder work nicely.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup natural peanut butter, or almond butter
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons ground flaxseed, or protein powder
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds, or protein powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract, optional
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt (if using unsalted nut butter), optional
  • 1/4 cup mini chocolate chips, optional

Steps

  1. Gather the ingrediets
  2. Line a rimmed baking sheet with parchment paper
  3. In a medium mixing bowl, combine the rolled oats, peanut butter, honey, ground flax, chia seeds, vanilla(if using). Use a wodden spoon or fork to mix well
  4. If you're using chocalate chips, add them and mix to combine
  5. Scoop out the mixture using a spoon or cookie scoop and use your hands to roll it into a smooth ball, then transfer to the prepated baking sheet. Repeat with the remaining mixture.
  6. Serve immediately or store the protein balls in the fridge in an airtight container until ready to eat.
